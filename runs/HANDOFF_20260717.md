1. The court-margin 1.0m gate runs on POOL detections, **pre-association**. The bridge is created

   above any deployed floor. **Raising the floor makes it strictly WORSE**: more real far-player
   dropouts → more gaps → more/longer bridges. The preregistered conf-0.30 arm proved this.
3. The speed guard **scales with gap length**: `merge_distance_slack_m (1.25) + max_gap_fill_speed_m_s

   of allowance — more than the 10.4 m the bridge travelled. **A per-gap DISTANCE cap does not exist.**
4. Appearance evidence **could not veto**: a missing embedding degrades to a neutral 0.5 cost inside
   a weighted cost soup, never a hard refusal. Synthetic frames legitimately carry no embedding.
